DMG

Damage types.

Values

Name Description
DMG_GENERIC Generic damage.
DMG_CRUSH Crush damage. By falling or moving object.
DMG_BULLET Bullet. Shot.
DMG_SLASH Slash. Cut, clawed, stabbed.
DMG_BURN Burn. Heat burned.
DMG_FREEZE Freeze. Frozen.
DMG_FALL Fall. Fell too far.
DMG_BLAST Blast. Explosive blast damage.
DMG_CLUB Club. Crowbar, punch, headbutt.
DMG_SHOCK Shock. Electric shock.
DMG_SONIC Sonic. Sound pulse shockwave.
DMG_ENERGYBEAM Energy beam. Laser or other high energy beam.
DMG_NEVERGIB Never gib. With this bit OR'd in, no damage type will be able to gib victims upon death.
DMG_ALWAYSGIB Always gib. With this bit OR'd in, any damage type can be made to gib victims upon death.
DMG_LAUNCH Launch. Launch into air.
DMG_DROWN Drown. Drowning.
DMG_TIMEBASED Mash for time-based damage.
DMG_PARALYZE Paralyze. Slows affected creature down.
DMG_NERVEGAS Nerve gas. Nerve toxins, very bad.
DMG_POISON Poison. Blood poisoning.
DMG_RADIATION Radiation. Radiation exposure.
DMG_DROWNRECOVER Drown recover. Drowning recovery.
DMG_ACID Acid. Toxic chemicals or acid burns.
DMG_SLOWBURN Slow burn. In an oven.
DMG_SLOWFREEZE Slow freeze. In a subzero freezer.
DMG_MORTAR Mortar. Hit by air raid (done to distinguish grenade from mortar)
DMG_SNIPER Sniper. Hit by a Sniper.
DMG_MEDKITHEAL Medkit heal. Medkit healing.
DMG_SHOCK_GLOW Shock glow. Flag for shock glow effect.
DMG_GIB_CORPSE Gib corpse. These are the damage types that are allowed to gib corpses.
DMG_SHOWNHUD Shown on HUD. These are the damage types that have clien hud art.
